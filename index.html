MARVEL CINEMATIC UNIVERSE

Marvel movies have always been a huge part of my life. Every film they’ve made is truly a chef’s kiss and always leaves me amazed. Here are my top five favorites.

THOR RAGNAROK

The best MCU movies . I love every character especially Hella. She had so much potential only defeated by Surtur together with the destruction of Asgard.

AVENGERS INFINITY WAR

What I love about Infinity War is that this is where Thanos had his first appearance. The battle here are insane and I love every details in the movie.

AVENGERS END GAME

This movie brings together many iconic MCU characters, and it is absolutely worth watching. The final battle between Thanos and the Avengers was incredibly intense and gave me goosebumps.

MULTIVERSE OF MADNESS

I really love how Wanda Maximoff evolved into one of the most powerful characters in the MCU. Watching every movie allows you to fully understand her story and growth. She, along with Doctor Strange, are my favorite characters.

SHANG-CHI

I really love the storyline of this movie. The characters are fantastic, especially Shang-Chi. The fight between him and his father, Wenwu, was intense and beautifully done.
